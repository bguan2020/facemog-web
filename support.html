Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported?

FaceMog requires an iPhone with a TrueDepth camera (iPhone X or later) running iOS 17 or later.

How do I get the best scan?

Hold your phone about 12–14 inches from your face in a well-lit environment. Stay still and follow the on-screen guidance. Avoid strong backlighting or direct sunlight.

What are scan credits?

Each face analysis uses one scan credit. You can purchase credits through the App Store. Credits do not expire.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the App, go to the purchase screen, and your purchases will be automatically restored via your Apple ID. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual scans within the app. To request deletion of any server-side data, email us at brianguan98@gmail.com.

Is my face data safe?

Yes. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. Scan data is automatically deleted from our servers after 30 days. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
