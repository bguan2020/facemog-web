Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Nuanced Labs Inc. ("we", "us", "our") operates the FaceMog mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Face Scan Data. FaceMog uses your device's TrueDepth camera to capture a 3D scan of your face. This produces a 3D mesh (geometry) and a color texture image. This data is sent to our servers for analysis and is used solely to generate your face analysis results.

Purchase Information. If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. We only receive a confirmation of your purchase to unlock features.

Locally Stored Data. Your scan results and thumbnails are stored locally on your device so you can revisit them. This data stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information — the app does not require an account.
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV).
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not serve advertisements.

3. How We Use Your Data

Face scan data is uploaded to our secure servers (hosted on Amazon Web Services) exclusively to perform the 3D face analysis. Once processing is complete, results are returned to your device. We do not use your face data for any other purpose, including training machine learning models.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. If you choose to use the "Share" feature, a link to your analysis is generated that you can send to others at your discretion.

5. Data Retention

Scan data uploaded for processing is retained on our servers for up to 30 days to allow you to access your results, after which it is automatically deleted. Locally stored scans remain on your device until you delete them.

6. Data Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Scan files are stored in encrypted cloud storage with access restricted to our processing pipeline.

7. Children's Privacy

FaceMog is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will delete any associated data.

8. Your Rights

You can delete your locally stored scans at any time from within the app. To request deletion of any server-side data associated with your scans, contact us at the email below and we will process your request promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
